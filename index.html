Welcome to Viz!

Our mission is to provide convenient access to data visualizations accross the world-wide web!

Currently, we are carrying weather data from a list of randomly generated cities. More precisely, we are visualizing relationships between a city's latitude and weather features. Our goal is to analyze the change in weather as you get closer to the equator.
