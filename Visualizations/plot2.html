Lat / Humidity

This plot describes the relationship between a given city's latitude, and humidity level. The relationship is relatively constant, with some clustering analysis that could potentially be utilized to achieve a more detailed analysis of this data. However currently, there seems to be little to no relationship between lat and humidity levels for a given city.
